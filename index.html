Know Your Ingredients

Electrolyte Balance & Osmoregulation

A precise blend of sodium, potassium, magnesium and calcium to keep fluid levels stable, muscles firing and nerves responsive.

Sodium Chloride (NaCl)

Primary extracellular ion for fluid balance and nerve transmission. Essential for maintaining blood pressure and cellular hydration.

Potassium Chloride (KCl)

Supports cardiac rhythm and neuromuscular activity. Critical for heart function and muscle contraction.

Magnesium Lactate

Cofactor in 300+ biochemical reactions. Helps prevent cramps and fatigue while supporting energy production.

Calcium Carbonate

Stabilises cell membranes and aids muscle contraction. Essential for bone health and nerve signaling.

Trisodium Citrate

Buffers lactic acid for smoother endurance performance. Helps maintain optimal pH during intense activity.
