— 07 / Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions.

Everything you need to know about Flow Hydration.

What is Flow Hydration?
Flow Hydration is a precision-engineered electrolyte drink mix designed for optimal hydration. It contains a balanced blend of sodium, potassium, magnesium, and calcium with just 6.9g of glucose for quick energy – perfect for athletes, fitness enthusiasts, and anyone looking to upgrade their daily hydration.
How do I use Flow?
Simply tear open one sachet and mix it with 500ml of cold water. Stir or shake until fully dissolved, and enjoy! Use it before, during, or after workouts, or anytime you need to stay hydrated throughout the day.
Does Flow contain sugar?
Flow contains 6.9g of glucose per sachet for quick energy absorption during activity. We use zero artificial sweeteners – just natural flavoring for clean, effective hydration that fuels your performance.
How long does delivery take?

Can I cancel my order?

How should I store Flow sachets?
Store your Flow sachets in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Once mixed with water, consume within 24 hours for best taste and efficacy. Keep sachets sealed until ready to use.
Is Flow suitable for everyone?
Flow is designed for adults looking to enhance their hydration. If you have any medical conditions, are pregnant, nursing, or taking medications, please consult your healthcare provider before use. Not recommended for children.
