What exactly is in a Flow sachet?
A precise blend of sodium, potassium, magnesium, calcium and trisodium citrate, with 6.9g of glucose for fast absorption. Zero artificial sweeteners, zero added colors.
How and when should I drink it?
One sachet in 500ml cold water. Stir until dissolved. Drink before, during, or after activity — or any time you need to upgrade plain water.
Does Flow contain sugar?
6.9g of glucose per sachet, used purely to drive electrolyte absorption (the sodium-glucose co-transport mechanism). No artificial sweeteners.
